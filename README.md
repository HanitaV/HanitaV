# Hi there 👋, I'm Harry Ha Noi! 

**Mechatronics Engineering Student @ UTT | Developer | Android Modder | Designer**

Welcome to my GitHub! I am a student majoring in **Mechatronics (Công nghệ Kỹ thuật Cơ điện tử)** at the **University of Transport Technology (UTT)**. I am passionate about bridging the gap between hardware and software, from system-level Android modifications to building web platforms for student communities.

### 👨‍💻 About Me

- 🎓 Studying Mechatronics at UTT, combining technical hardware knowledge with software engineering.
- 🚀 **Developer of NEO (New Era Orientation):** Building and maintaining a community platform designed to support students with IT exams and university orientation.
- 📱 **Android Enthusiast:** Deeply involved in Android system modifications, including ROM porting, bootloader unlocking, and kernel building (working with SukiSu and SUSFS).
- 💡 **Hackathon & Tech Event Active:** Participated in **GDG DevFest Hanoi** (focusing on Multi-Agent Architecture), Dataflow from Hamic ( VNU-US), Vietnamese Student HackAIthon and GDG on Campus Hackathons.
- 🎨 **Creative Media:** I handle graphic design, logo creation, and video production for UTT's Club of Hanoi Communication (**CHC**), including major events like the UTT Student Congress.
